Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information while using the wBadget application — a service for managing personal finances.

1. Information Collection

We may collect minimal data necessary for the app’s operation, including:

  • Transaction data (amounts, categories, dates, types);
  • User preferences and settings.

We do not store any personal or confidential data without your consent.

2. Use of Information

All collected information is used solely for:

  • Displaying and analyzing your financial data within the app;
  • Improving functionality and user experience;
  • Generating usage statistics.

3. Security

We take all necessary technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. All data is stored locally on your device.

4. User Rights

  • You can delete your data from the app at any time.
  • You may contact us via the developer’s email to request deletion or clarification of your data.

5. Use of Third-Party Services

This app does not contain, display, or access any third-party content. If such services are added in the future, users will be notified separately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. All changes will be published on this page with the updated date indicated.
